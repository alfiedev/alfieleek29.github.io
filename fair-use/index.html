Our hosting services are designed to provide you with a high-quality hosting experience. To ensure fair use of our services and prevent abuse, we have established the following fair use policy.

Prohibited Activities

You may not use our hosting services for any of the following activities:

  • Hosting or distributing any content that is illegal or infringes upon the intellectual property rights of others.
  • Sending or promoting spam, malware, or other malicious content.
  • Hosting or distributing any content that promotes or engages in hate speech, discrimination, or harassment.
  • Engaging in any activity that interferes with the operation of our hosting services or the servers that support them.
  • Using our hosting services to engage in any fraudulent or deceptive activity.

Monitoring & Enforcement

We may monitor your use of our hosting services to ensure compliance with this fair use policy. If we become aware of any prohibited activity, we may take the following actions:

  • Suspend or terminate your hosting account.
  • Remove or block any content that violates this fair use policy.
  • Take any other actions we deem necessary to prevent further violations or harm to our hosting services or other users.

Modification to Fair Use Policy

We may modify this fair use policy from time to time to reflect changes in our hosting services or applicable laws and regulations. We will notify you of any significant changes and provide you with the opportunity to review the revised policy before continuing to use our services.
